Job Overview

As a Full-Stack Engineer at Visarun, you will join a small, highly focused product team building an AI-powered visa management platform for tourist and short-term business travel.

Our product combines:
Structured decision logic (1,000+ workflow paths) Automated document validation Real-time status tracking AI-powered user guidance Multi-role dashboards and workflow orchestration

This is a workflow-heavy, logic-intensive SaaS platform — not a simple CRUD app.

In this role, you will:
✓ Build and maintain both backend and frontend systems

✓ Design complex features that interact with AI-driven decision flows

You will have strong ownership and direct impact on both technical architecture and product direction.

We operate in an Agile environment and actively use modern AI tools to enhance both development efficiency and product capabilities.
